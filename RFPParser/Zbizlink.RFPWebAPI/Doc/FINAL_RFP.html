II.        PURPOSE/OBJECTIVE

 

The purpose of this RFP is to request sealed proposals from experienced and qualified Offerors/Proposers with expertise in expeditiously sourcing, screening, and providing top quality candidates to fill CCPS’ temporary staffing needs on an “as-needed” basis, as determined by the district.  

 

CCPS also reserves the right to cancel the RFP, to make an aggregate award, or to split the award between more than one Offeror/Proposer.

 

There is no minimum level of usage for any resulting Contract(s) under this RFP.

 

III.      INTRODUCTION AND BACKGROUND

 

Charles County Public Schools is one of the fastest growing school systems in Maryland, with a population of 157,705. Charles County, Maryland is located less than 30 miles south of Washington, D.C. and considered the gateway to Southern Maryland.  For the 2019-20 school year, the district has 37 schools and three (3) educational centers. For the SY 201819, approximately 27,108 students attending these schools and educational centers.  

 

The mission of Charles County Public Schools is to provide an opportunity for all schoolaged children to receive an academically challenging, quality education that builds character, equips for leadership and prepares for life, in an environment that is safe and conducive to learning. 

 

CCPS has 3,682 employees (teachers, support and technical staff, and administrators) to serve the students in various capacities. Of the 3,682 employees, 3,620 (98.3%) are fulltime employees and 62 (1.7%) are part-time employees.  CCPS has approximately 1,993 temporary employees, as of the writing of this solicitation; however, this number fluctuates and more than 80 percent of all employees live in Charles County.

VI.       EVALUATION PROCESS

recommend the highest ranked Vendor/Contractor(s) for award. CCPS may request oral interviews from the highest ranked Vendor(s)/Contractor(s) with the committee. 1.                  Qualifications and Experience – The firm’s overall capability and experience to provide quality temporary staffing candidates and services. 

 

2.                  Background Checks – The ability of the firm to conduct background checks and investigations as required by this the State of Maryland’s HB 486 and this RFP;  

 

3.                  References –  Feedback when requested by CCPS from your current and past customers relative to temporary staffing services for public school systems; and

 

4.                  Pricing – Pricing will be evaluated based on the average hourly rates of Temporary Workers categories offered.  Only those temporary Worker categories that are the same from firm to firm will be evaluated.
